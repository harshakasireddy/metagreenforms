Our Story

Commitment to Green Excellence

Founded in 2018 by a team of landscape architects and workforce management specialists, Verdant Works was created to fill a critical gap in the industry: the need for integrated solutions. We believe that a beautiful, sustainable design is only as good as the reliable, skilled hands that bring it to life.

Our dual focus ensures project continuity, budget adherence, and superior quality control from the initial sketch to the final planting. We manage the design, the materials, and the people, so you only have one point of contact. This streamlined approach is what defines our success and the satisfaction of our commercial and high-end residential clients.

Our Mission

  • Sustainability in every design.
  • Uncompromising labor safety standards.
  • Single-source accountability for all projects.
